Eat around the world without leaving Berlin!

​

Berlin has a huge diversity of people who have made this city their new home, bringing with them their food traditions from all around the world. Here restaurants range from Mexican, Russian, Italian, Turkish, Iranian, Vietnamese, Indian, Cameroonian to Balkan and beyond. Looking for the best place to eat can be a bit overwhelming, so this article has the purpose of making your choice easier. Below you can find the Top 10 restaurants in Berlin, which can satisfy every taste and every craving.

1. Malafemmena

​

Malafemmena is probably the best pizzeria in Berlin that serves real Neapolitan pizza. It is not just like any other pizza you have ever had, it is a pizza that is prepared with the freshest Italian ingredients and cooked in a special traditional oven. You can't make a mistake with any pick from the menu. From starters to desserts everything is home made and will make you lick your fingers. You can find Malafemmena in Schöneberg and in Prenzlauer Berg. Make sure to reserve a table here as it tends to be quite crowded.

​
​

2. Da jia le

This is probably the most authentic Chinese restaurant I have ever tried in Berlin. They have amazing and tasty sichuan meals and a great selection of beer! My personal recommendation would be the Fish stew Sichuan style. It is located in Moabit. Menu and address: here!

​

3. BIP - Bier in Pankow

​

In this restaurant you can try traditional Bulgarian cuisine. Bulgarian food often incorporates salads as appetizers, soups and pastries such as banitsa. Main courses are very typically water-based stews, either vegetarian or with lamb, goat meat, veal, chicken or pork. The cold yogurt soup called Tarator is among Bulgarians favorite foods for the summer. Don't miss it out if you visit the place in a hot day. BIP is located in Pankow (Kreuzstraße 14, 13187 Berlin). There is no website, but you can make a reservation by calling +49 30 4410226.

4. Finnegan's Irish Pub

A chill place to be with friends when there is a football game. A great Irish pub with tasty food, I especially recommend their pies! It is located in Steglitz. Find out more here.

5. Chupenga Burritos & Salads

Chupenga is a little gem if you are looking for delicious Mexican food. Everything is fresh and you can choose from a variety of ingredients to create your own meal. The portions are generous. The home made guacamole and different lemonades are definitely a must, but also don't forget their unbeatable burritos! You can visit theit website here and chose which one of their 4 locations to visit (Gendarmenmarkt, Checkpoint Charlie, Eastside Gallery or Friedrichstrasse).

​

6. Seri Melayu

A nice atmosphere in the evening, friendly restaurant and tasty and authentic malaysian food, absolutely recommend their Nasi lemak! You can find this restaurant in Charlottenburg. More infos here.

7. Cèdre blanc

CÈDRE BLANC is an excellent Lebanese restaurant where you can eat delicious and authentic food. Lebanese wines are also something to taste in here! If you're looking for a place where you can just sit and taste very good humus (especially the beetroot one...so appetizing ), cooked rice with different sorts of meet and vegetable, this is the place you have to go in Berlin. It is located close to Charlottenburg Palace. You can find their website here.

​

8. Makenene

A great place to try delicious cameroonian food in Charlottenburg! You have to try their grilled fish with plantains! Here is their website.

​
​

9. Taverna Athene

This is one of the best Greek restaurants you can find in Berlin. The taste of their Moussaka will bring you back to Santorini or Mykonos... If you decide to visit this authentic restaurant you will be welcomed with a shot of Ouzo and a great mood. For those of you who like meat we would recommend not to miss the pork Gyros and the grilled Octopus. Don't worry if you are vegetarian as there are plenty of fresh salads and veggie appetizers. You can also enjoy the excellent wine made in sunny Greece. Taverna Athene is located in Friedrichshain-Kreuzberg (Borough). You can visit their website here.
